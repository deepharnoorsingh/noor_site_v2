Why Homeowners Choose Noor Projects for Stone Work

Professional stone work starts below the surface. We excavate to the right depth, install and compact the base in lifts, set accurate slopes for drainage, and finish with polymeric sand. The result: crisp lines, smooth transitions, and surfaces that don’t heave or trip you up.

Rock‑Solid Base Preparation

We dig to spec and install a compacted road‑base in layers. That foundation keeps patios and paths flat, helps water move, and prevents the wavy, uneven look that shows up when base is rushed.

Clean Layouts & Tight Lines

Your stonework should read crisp from every angle. We use string lines, spacers, and detailed cutting so bond lines stay straight and curves look intentional—not jagged.

Freeze–Thaw Resilience

Calgary’s winters are tough on hardscapes. We set proper slopes, install edge restraints, and lock joints with polymeric sand so your patio resists movement and your walkway doesn’t open up.

Drainage That Works

Standing water shortens the life of any surface. We grade the base and surface to shed water and integrate simple solutions (like channel placement or minor swales) where needed.

Right Material, Right Place

We’ll guide you on paver thickness for traffic, slip‑resistant textures for steps, and colors that complement your home. Natural stone or concrete pavers—done with purpose.

Low‑Maintenance Finish

We set and activate polymeric sand correctly to reduce weeds and keep joints tidy. You get a cleaner look and less maintenance through the season.
