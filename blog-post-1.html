Sod Aftercare: 7-Day Success Checklist

Watering, first mow, and soil tips to lock in that new‑lawn look.

Day 1–3: Water Like Clockwork

Keep the sod moist but not swampy. Aim for 2–3 light waterings per day in hot weather.

Day 4–6: Check Seams & Edges

Gently press lifted edges, add topsoil if needed, and reduce watering frequency.

Day 7: First Mow

Set mower high and take off no more than 1/3 of the blade length.
