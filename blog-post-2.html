Remove fixtures, prep surfaces, and complete plumbing/electrical rough‑ins.

Week 2: Tile & Waterproofing

Backer board, waterproof membranes, and tile setting with proper curing.

Week 3: Fixtures & Finish

Install vanity, toilet, trims, and final silicone. Punchlist and clean.
