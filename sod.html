Whether you’re replacing patchy turf or finishing a new build, we deliver clean, durable results that boost curb appeal.

Mowing That Supports Growth

We guide you on first‑mow timing (usually once the sod is rooted) and set proper mow heights for Kentucky bluegrass blends common in Calgary. Our recommendations reduce stress, strengthen roots, and help your lawn thicken over time—so it looks better and performs better after every cut.

Targeted Fertilization Plans That Work

Healthy lawns need more than water. We outline a simple, seasonal fertilizer schedule (spring starter, mid‑season boost, and fall winterizer) that supports density and color without overloading nitrogen. We can supply products or integrate with your preferred brand.

Aeration & Overseeding for Dense, Resilient Turf

Core aeration relieves compaction from our dry climate and heavy foot traffic, improving oxygen and nutrient flow. Overseeding fills thin spots and enhances variety for resilience. We time these services around your irrigation schedule and daylight conditions for better germination.

Smart Watering & Drainage Insights

New sod thrives with consistent moisture, not constant saturation. We provide a week‑by‑week watering plan, adjust for shade/sun exposure, and check slope to prevent pooling. For tricky areas, we can recommend minor grading fixes or french drains to protect your investment.

Seasonal Clean‑Ups & Proactive Maintenance

Spring and fall clean‑ups remove debris that can smother turf and invite pests. We pair clean‑ups with edge touch‑ups, spot topdressing, and selective weed control to keep your sod even, breathable, and photo‑ready from May through October.

Flexible Service Plans Built Around You

Choose one‑time installation or ongoing care packages that include fertilizing, aeration, and seasonal checks. We work across Calgary & surrounding areas, coordinate around your schedule, and provide clear communication and warranties so you always know what’s happening on your property.

Seasonal Lawn Maintenance

Our team ensures your lawn is ready for every season and recovers from the last.

Spring

Kickstart your lawn’s growth with debris cleanup, aeration, and topdressing where needed. We prepare soil for strong root development and set a watering schedule that ensures your sod takes hold quickly.

Summer

Maintain lush, vibrant grass with proper mowing height, consistent watering, and mid-season fertilization. Our summer care keeps your turf dense, green, and resistant to heat stress.

Fall

Prepare your lawn for winter with a final mow, leaf cleanup, and targeted fertilization. We also aerate and overseed to fill bare spots, ensuring a healthier, thicker lawn in the next growing season.
