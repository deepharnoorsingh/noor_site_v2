Fence & Deck Installation Process

A clean, safe build from the ground up — precise layout, solid structure, and a finish that looks intentional.

1

On‑Site Consultation & Layout

We confirm property lines and utilities, measure grades, and map clear layouts for posts, spans, stairs, and gates. From there, we provide a practical design and scope.

2

Materials & Hardware

Pressure‑treated, cedar, or composite options. We’ll recommend fasteners, brackets, posts, and coatings that match your budget and maintenance goals.

3

Footings, Framing & Structure

Posts set plumb in proper footings; beams and joists installed to spec with hangers and hardware. We keep everything square, level, and secure before surface boards go on.

4

Boards, Railings & Finish

Tight board spacing, clean cuts, and safe, sturdy railings. We sand edges, align fasteners, and review coatings or stain options for long‑term protection.
